Signals

A speculative multisensory fragrance system that translates scent into visual and motion based design. The visual language is mathematically derived from EEG data — mapping scent profiles to their corresponding neural signatures. Scent is not described, but expressed through colour, form, and behaviour across physical, digital and spatial layers.
